## Key Insights from Human Notes

**Practice Evolution**: Fundamental shift from technical feasibility ("Can I do it?") to strategic alignment ("How to understand client outcomes and constraints"). With AI handling technical execution, consultant value centers on requirements translation, business context, and outcome optimization.

**Mental Model**: "I'm the manager, copilot is my developer" with human-in-the-loop approach. Human provides context, constraints, and quality control; AI provides rapid implementation and technical execution.

**Cognitive Liberation**: "When AI frees up your mental bandwidth, you don't get lazy - you get strategic" (with personality-dependent caveats). Mental energy redirects toward business strategy, client outcomes, and innovation when routine technical tasks are AI-handled.
